Phone Camera

The first camera phone is believed to be Japan’s Kyocera VP-210 launched in May 1999. With a 0.11MP camera and a storage capacity of 20 pictures, this device became the first mobile phone that had a built-in camera. It even came with a stand for users to take photos of themselves efficiently.

Source: Callaham, John. “The First Camera Phone Was Sold 22 Years Ago, and It’S Not What You’D Expect.” Android Authority, 20 Jan. 2024, www.androidauthority.com/first-camera-phone-anniversary-993492.
