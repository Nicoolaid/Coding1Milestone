Compact Digital Camera

The first compact digital camera was created by Fujifilm in 1988 with the name of FUJIX DS-1F. Storing its data through a memory card, the digital camera was able to produce completely digital images. This innovation sparked many other digital cameras to be made, each building onto the original model.
